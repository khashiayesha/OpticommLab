About Us

Optical Communication Laboratory

The Optical Communication Laboratory is a research laboratory focused on optical communication technology and fiber optic (FTTH) systems.

VISION

To become an internationally accredited Telecommunications Engineering undergraduate program actively involved in the development of education, research, and entrepreneurship in the field of digital telecommunications engineering.

MISSION

To provide an excellent and internationally accredited education program that includes and accommodates entrepreneurship initiatives. To develop and disseminate telecommunications science and technology, especially in wireless communication, recognized globally. To organize entrepreneurship programs among the academic community, focusing on technology-based telecommunications innovations. To apply telecommunications science and technology for the betterment of national civilization and the global community.

OUR CONTRIBUTIONS

Research & Projects

Automated Monitoring System for Rainwater Harvesting Tank at Telkom University

This research aims to develop a remote monitoring device based on the Internet of Things (IoT) that monitors pH values, water surface height, dissolved substance content, and water clarity levels in ground tanks.

Basic research study of flood detection early warning system based on Optical LiDAR integration of solar energy and Internet of Things

Research Scheme for Research for Beginner Lecturers Period 1 Wave 4 Fiscal Year 2025 Telkom University

Intelligent decoding using convolutional neural networks for long-distance optical communication in free space

Internal fund research for the 2025 fiscal year, period 1, Telkom University - non-cooperative research scheme.
